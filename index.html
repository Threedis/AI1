How it works

Four steps to your Bonus Register

Follow these steps to calculate statutory bonus for your employees under the Payment of Bonus Act, 1965. The tool handles eligibility, minimum wages, and LOP pro-ration automatically.

1
📋
Prepare Your Salary Register
Ensure your Excel file has these columns:
Emp ID · Employee Name · Designation · Basic (Th) · VDA (Th) · Sal Cal Days · Pay Days · Location · Month/Year
2
📤
Upload Monthly Files
Upload one file per month (Apr–Mar financial year). You can upload all 12 months at once. Files must be in .xlsx format. Drag & drop or click to browse.
3
⚡
Calculate
Click Calculate Bonus Register. The tool auto-detects each employee's Area (A/B/C), Skill level, and applicable Construction MW rate. Bonus is computed at 8.33% of prorated MW.
4
⬇
Review & Download
Filter by month, area, or eligibility. Use the month picker to view specific periods. Click Download Excel to export a compliance-ready bonus register.

Key Rules Applied Automatically

✅
Eligibility CheckBasic (Th) + VDA (Th) must be > ₹0 and < ₹21,000/month to qualify for bonus under the Act.
📍
Area ClassificationEmployee's location is auto-mapped to Area A, B, or C as per CLC city schedule, determining the applicable minimum wage rate.
🔧
Skill MappingDesignation is mapped to Unskilled / Semi-Skilled / Skilled / Highly Skilled using the Construction & Maintenance schedule (CLC).
📅
Period-wise MWMinimum wages are picked from the correct 6-month CLC period (Apr–Sep or Oct–Mar) based on the month of the salary register.
📐
LOP Pro-rationMinimum wages are prorated: MW ÷ Sal Cal Days × Pay Days, then floored (ROUNDDOWN). Accounts for Loss of Pay days automatically.
💰
Bonus CalculationBonus = FLOOR(Prorated MW × 8.33%). Applied only to eligible employees. Ineligible employees show — in the bonus column.
ℹ️ File format tip: The tool auto-detects the correct sheet and header row. Month/Year can be in the filename (e.g. April_2024.xlsx) or in a dedicated column. Rows with item counts (e.g. Feb/Item:06) and month-header rows are automatically skipped.
